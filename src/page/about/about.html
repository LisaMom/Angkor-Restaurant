Discover Cambodia

A land of ancient wonders,
culture, and natural beauty.

Cambodia is one of Southeast Asia’s most fascinating destinations, rich in history and cultural heritage. At its heart lies Angkor Wat, the largest religious monument in the world, showcasing the grandeur of the ancient Khmer Empire.

Beyond the temples, Cambodia offers a wide range of experiences — from the vibrant capital of Phnom Penh to the peaceful charm of Siem Reap, and the stunning white-sand beaches of Koh Rong. Visitors can explore floating villages, lush jungles, and lively local markets.

What truly makes Cambodia unforgettable is its people. Warm, welcoming, and deeply connected to their traditions, Cambodians offer visitors not just a destination, but a meaningful cultural experience.

"Travel Cambodia not just to see its beauty, but to feel its history, culture, and spirit." "

— Cambodia Tourism

Our journey

Where it all began

10
2010 Founded

Mom Lisa founds the company from a small office in Phnom Penh with one goal — to share the soul of Cambodia with the world. First trip sold: a 5-day Angkor Wat and Siem Reap cultural tour.

12
2012 Expansion

We expand beyond Siem Reap — adding Battambang, Kampot, and Kep to our route collection. Ban Sethdarasak and Chom Nadaraguel join the team, bringing the crew to 10 passionate locals.

14
2014 Award

Recognised as Best Local Tour Operator by the Cambodia Tourism Federation. We reach 2,000 travellers and launch our first community homestay programme in Mondulkiri.

17
2017 Sustainability

Launched our Responsible Travel Pledge — eliminating single-use plastics on all tours, supporting local Khmer guides exclusively, and donating to wildlife conservation in the Cardamom Mountains.

20
2020 Resilience

Navigated the global travel shutdown with zero redundancies. We used the downtime to develop new hidden-gem itineraries across Ratanakiri, Kratie, and the remote Mekong islands — ready for when the world opened again.

22
2022 Revival

Tourism bounces back and so do we. We welcome our 10,000th traveller, open a second office in Siem Reap, and launch our signature Mekong Slow Boat experience — now one of our most beloved journeys.

2025 Today

5 passionate team members. 15,000+ travellers across Cambodia. 20 curated local destinations. Proudly 100% Khmer-owned and operated — and Mom Lisa still personally welcomes every group on day one.
